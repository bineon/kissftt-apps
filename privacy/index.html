Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

KISSFTT apps are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

MDViewer

MDViewer operates entirely on your device. It reads Markdown files you choose to open and stores your recent file history and reading position locally on your device using standard iOS storage. No data is sent to any server.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage analytics, or telemetry data. Our apps do not contain any third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@kissftt.com.
